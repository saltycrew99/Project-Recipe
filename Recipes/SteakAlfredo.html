Steak Alfredo

If you've ever seen the movie Ratatouille, you'd know that even a rat can cook. For the sake of example, we will now be transformed into rats. Lets show those Italians that us rats can cook!

Ingredients

  • 1 pound of steak of choice
  • 1 box of fettuccine noodles
  • Alfredo sauce of choice (Bertolli is bomb for me)
  • Salt, pepper, paprika, italian seasoning and garlic powder
  • 2 tablespoons of butter
  • Grated parmesan

Steps

  1. First you'll want to get a good size pot filled with water, set it to a boil, and add the noodles. Once noodles are added, sprinkle about a tablespoon of italian seasoning.
  2. Now heat up your pan to about 65%. Add the butter and once it's melted add the steak of choice. For better flavor and crust season the steak before putting it on the pan with salt, pepper, and garlic powder.
  3. Strain noodles once they've cooked for about 7-8 minutes. While it strains, take steak out after 5-6 minutes on each side and cut it fajita style.
  4. Place steak and noodles together in pan and add the alfredo sauce. Once mixed together, add about 1/2 tablespoon of paprika and the same for italian seasoning.
  5. Sprinkle some of the parmesan on top and enjoy your GD masterpiece my guy!
