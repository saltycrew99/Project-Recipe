Chicken Fried Rice

What an adventure we have ahead of us today. Not only will your significant other thank you for feading them food from the God's, you may even ascend to them yourselves.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound of chicken thighs
  • 1 cup of brown rice
  • 2 cups water
  • 1 cup of peas and carrots mix
  • 1 egg
    • 1/2 cup of Soy sauce (I prefer low sodium) 2 tablespoons of butter Salt, pepper, and garlic powder

Steps

  1. First, you'll want to get your brown rice going in a small sauce pan. Pour 2 cups of water to 1 cup of rice and cook. Once rice looks soaked enough, let sit for about 20 minutes.
  2. Then heat up a frying pan to about 60% heat. While heating up, add a pinch of salt, pepper, and garlic to each side of chicken thighs.
  3. Place the chicken into the pan and cook each side for about 5-6 minutes or until a good crust forms on each side.
  4. Take the chicken out and cut into smaller pieces. Throw the chicken back in the pan with the brown rice.
  5. Add peas and carrots mix with a few dashes of soy sauce to coat the rice into a darker shade.
  6. Once everything has cooked together, add butter and stir. Once butter is mostly melted, add the egg and scramble.
  7. Viola, enjoy the mouth watering recipe of Chicken Fried Rice from Ghandi himself (not really).
